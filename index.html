About ACM PCCoE

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, bringing together educators, researchers, and students to foster excellence in computing.

ACM PCCoE Student Chapter organizes events to promote peer learning, networking, and skills development.
